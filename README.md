## 🏆 Our Interview Process

We respect your time. Here's exactly what to expect — no surprises:

```
1️⃣  Intro Call        (30 min)  — Chat with our recruiter, get your questions answered
2️⃣  Technical Screen  (60 min)  — Real problems, real conversations (no LeetCode grind)
3️⃣  Team Interview    (90 min)  — Meet the people you'd work with daily
4️⃣  Offer! 🎉         (fast)    — We move quickly once we find the right person
```
